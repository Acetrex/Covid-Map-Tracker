What is Covid-19?

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus.

Browse COVID-19 Information →
Why is some data displaying as 0 or None?

This data can be displaying in this way since there was no data collected for that day.

How to print the map?

The map can be printed by looking for the icon that looks like a print symbol above the map legend. You can then click on this and click to the icon to the right of it, which will display a new window. You can now select where to print to or save it as a PDF file.

When was the last day of the data taken?

The last date of the data was taken on 2021-12-01. It will get updated on a weekly basis from then.
